How-to



  1. Login to Dominos Pizza Malaysia GPS Tracker.
  2. Navigate until you can see your order with blinking current status:

  3. Open browser control (F12 on most browsers) and go to Network tab. Then click on any requests in the list that resembles the order cart id. After that click Headers to get the Authorization Bearer token:

  4. To get the order cart id url, right click on the request and copy the url:

  5. With the obtained order cart id url and Authorization Bearer token, Track now!
